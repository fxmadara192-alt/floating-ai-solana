# LIQUIDITY

## Tokenomics
The FLOAT token is designed with sustainable tokenomics that encourage both usage and holding. Specific allocations will incentivize ecosystem growth and community engagement while ensuring liquidity.

## Liquidity Pools
Liquidity pools are integral to the FLOAT ecosystem, providing necessary liquidity for seamless trading and reducing price volatility. Participants can contribute to these pools in various ways, earning rewards in return for their participation.

## FLOAT Token Distribution
The distribution of FLOAT tokens will be strategically managed to promote long-term viability: 50% for community rewards, 25% for development, 15% for strategic partnerships, and 10% for the founding team. This balanced approach ensures that the interests of all stakeholders are aligned and encourages a healthy market dynamic.
